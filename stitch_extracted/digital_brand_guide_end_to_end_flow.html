# Akshara Type Studio: End-to-End Experience Map This document outlines the proposed user journey and screen architecture for the new Verdant Akshara digital presence. ## 1. The Entry Point: Brand Awareness * **Screen:** [Akshara Home: The Living Manuscript]({{DATA:SCREEN:SCREEN_10}}) * **Goal:** Establish premium positioning and cultural heritage through immersive parallax and "The Living Manuscript" creative direction. ## 2. Discovery: The Product Catalog * **Screen:** [Fonts Library: The Archive]({{DATA:SCREEN:SCREEN_13}}) * **Goal:** Allow users to browse the full collection of Kannada typefaces with glassmorphic filters and interactive previews. ## 3. Evaluation: Technical & Artistic Deep Dive * **Screen:** [Font Detail: Specimen Explorer]({{DATA:SCREEN:SCREEN_9}}) * **Goal:** Provide designers with the technical specs (Variable axes, Character Waterfall) needed to license a font. ## 4. Utility: Community & Professional Tools * **Screen:** [Type Lab: Digital Tools]({{DATA:SCREEN:SCREEN_11}}) * **Goal:** Modernize the "Tools" section from the old site into a high-end workbench for Phonetic Typing and OCR. ## 5. Engagement: Thought Leadership * **Screen:** [The Journal: Studio News]({{DATA:SCREEN:SCREEN_2}}) * **Goal:** Build authority in the typography space with editorial-style articles on variable axes and heritage. ## 6. Connection: Trust & Heritage * **Screen:** [About: The Story of Akshara]({{DATA:SCREEN:SCREEN_4}}) * **Goal:** Connect the modern digital work to the 1984 origins and traditional calligraphy roots. ## 7. Conversion: Custom Work & Inquiry * **Screen:** [Contact & Collaboration]({{DATA:SCREEN:SCREEN_12}}) * **Goal:** A frictionless, professional point of contact for high-end custom typeface commissions. --- **Comparison Reference:** [Original Website Screenshot]({{DATA:IMAGE:IMAGE_14}})
