Frequently Asked Licensing Questions

"ಪಾರದರ್ಶಕತೆ ನಮ್ಮ ಮೂಲಮಂತ್ರ - Transparency is our core value."

Can I upgrade my license later?

Yes. You can upgrade from Personal to Commercial or Enterprise by paying the difference within 6 months of the initial purchase.

Do the licenses expire?

All our licenses are perpetual. Once you purchase, you own the rights to use that version of the font forever within the scope of your license.

Are webfonts included in the desktop license?

Commercial licenses include both OTF/TTF (Desktop) and WOFF/WOFF2 (Web) formats at no extra cost.

Can I modify the glyphs?

Standard licenses do not allow direct modification of the font files. For custom modifications or exclusive weights, please contact the studio for an Enterprise quote.
